Surat, India: Traffic Accidents

An #EcoHack DC Project

Surat, a busy trading city in western India, is considered one of the many rapidly growing urban areas in the country. India suffers the maximum number of traffic casualties, and its cities bleed significantly. The city of Surat released traffic accident data for two of its busiest (and most dangerous, presumably) corridors.

A cursory look at the visualized data provides insights into the land use, and traffic patterns. For instance, the intersection that leads to the major industrial area attracts a large number of collisions. Reading the landscape in such a manner should help arrive at highly contextual solutions to make cities safer to commute in.
